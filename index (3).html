Common Questions

Frequently Asked

What is the return policy?
+
We offer a 30-day no-questions-asked return policy on all products. If you're not completely satisfied, simply contact our support team and we'll arrange a free return. Refunds are processed within 3–5 business days.
Do you offer international shipping?
+
Yes — we ship to over 80 countries worldwide. Standard international shipping takes 7–14 business days. Express options are available at checkout. All orders over $150 include free standard international shipping.
What warranty do the products carry?
+
All EBuds products come with a comprehensive 2-year manufacturer warranty covering defects in materials and workmanship. The CCA CA24 IEM also includes a lifetime cable replacement guarantee.
Are the earbuds compatible with all devices?
+
All wireless models use standard Bluetooth and are compatible with iOS, Android, Windows, and macOS. The TE-W1 Adaptive supports LDAC for Hi-Res audio on Android devices. The CCA CA24 is a wired IEM compatible with any 3.5mm or 4.4mm balanced output.
How does fast charging work?
+
Our fast-charge enabled models (TE-W1 and Rolosar Wireless) support rapid charging via USB-C. Just 10 minutes of charging provides up to 2 hours of playback — ideal when you're short on time before heading out.
Can I use these for calls and meetings?
+
Absolutely. All wireless models feature built-in microphones with ENC (Environmental Noise Cancellation) for call quality. The Rolosar Wireless uses dual-mic ENC specifically optimized for clear voice pickup in noisy environments.
